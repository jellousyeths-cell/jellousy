Proposed Solution

To solve the issue of poor internet connection during TLE classes, we propose setting up a separate router dedicated only to TLE use.

This reduces network traffic and allows smoother access to online TLE tools and platforms.

  • Improves internet speed during TLE classes
  • Reduces disconnections and lag
  • Ensures stable access to online resources
  • Allows better monitoring of TLE network usage

The success of this solution can be measured by fewer disconnections and more efficient completion of online TLE activities.
