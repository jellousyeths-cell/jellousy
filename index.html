The Problem

Many students experience poor internet connection during online TLE classes. Slow speeds and frequent disconnections make it difficult to complete activities and access online tools.

This issue affects learning performance, participation, and overall classroom productivity.

  • Interrupted online lessons
  • Delayed submission of activities
  • Difficulty accessing learning platforms
  • Increased stress for students and teachers
